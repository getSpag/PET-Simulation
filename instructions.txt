1. Download VSCode or cursor
2. Clone repo, open in VSCode / cursor
3. Install Extensions in extensions.json
    select a kit when CMake prompts (ex visual studio build tools x64)
4. Ensure CMake executable is on your PATH
5. Point clangd parameter
    --query-driver= c/c++ compiler in kit you selected when CMake asked you to select a kit

    in settings.json
    (for intellisense)


Run -- 
    windows: .\build-ninja\PET_new.exe 10 10
             .\build-ninja\PET.exe 10 10

    macos: ./build-ninja/PET_new 10 10
           ./build-ninja/PET 10 10
